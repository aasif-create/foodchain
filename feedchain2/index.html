Why a Web3 rail for food redistribution?

FeedChain uses Web3 where it matters: verifiable coordination, open data, and shared ownership ‚Äî not speculation.

Why Web3?

Food rescue already happens in every city ‚Äî but coordination is fragile. Spreadsheets, group chats, and siloed apps make it hard to know what was posted, who picked it up, and whether it reached people on time.

Web3 gives us a neutral coordination layer. Every event host, volunteer, and NGO writes to the same shared ledger, creating a live map of surplus food and its journey without relying on a single company or database.
